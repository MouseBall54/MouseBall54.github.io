## How To Write a Short Complaint

A short structured complaint usually works better than a long frustrated message.

1. State when auto-renewal happened and the amount involved.
2. State the promise or policy connected to early cancellation fee.
3. State one requested remedy: refund, replacement, repair, or charge reversal.
4. Attach evidence for transfer terms and use cancellation confirmation as the next deadline.

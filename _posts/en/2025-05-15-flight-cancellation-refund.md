## Why This Problem Happens

The core of Flight Cancellation Refund: Separate Vouchers From Cash Refunds is putting airline cancellation and voucher acceptance on the same timeline. Accepting a voucher can complicate a later request for cash refund. Without a record of refund request date, it becomes harder to decide whether to escalate to the seller, platform, or payment provider first.

The practical solution starts with a **short timeline and evidence folder**, not a long emotional explanation. When airline cancellation, voucher acceptance, and refund request date are on one page, the seller message and agency complaint can use almost the same facts.

- **Receipt and order number**: save transaction ID, payment method, and seller identity that prove airline cancellation.

## How To Write a Short Complaint

A short structured complaint usually works better than a long frustrated message.

1. State when airline cancellation happened and the amount involved.
2. State the promise or policy connected to voucher acceptance.
3. State one requested remedy: refund, replacement, repair, or charge reversal.
4. Attach evidence for refund request date and use payment method as the next deadline.

## Signals To Check First

- **serving size**: Record start date, duration, frequency, triggers, and relieving factors in the same format. Sudden worsening or safety concerns should move professional help first.
- **sauce amount**: Write the effect on daily function. Changes in school, work, sleep, eating, or mobility often matter more than a vague severity label.
- **processed food frequency**: Check age, pregnancy, existing conditions, medicines, recent infection, and injury. The same sign can mean different things under different conditions.
- **broth intake**: For breathing trouble, chest pain, confusion, severe bleeding, self-harm risk, or other emergency signs, seeking urgent help comes before record keeping.
